## 1. Purpose

This Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) establishes a consistent and repeatable method for preparing homemade pizza in a home kitchen setting. It ensures food quality, safety compliance, and efficient workflow management while producing two distinct pizza varieties: vegetarian and BBQ chicken.  

Following this SOP will result in professional-quality pizzas suitable for family dining or casual entertaining.

---

## 2. Scope and Objectives

### Scope
This SOP applies to individuals preparing pizza using standard kitchen equipment in a residential environment.

### Objectives
- Prepare fresh pizza dough from basic ingredients  
- Assemble two pizza varieties using correct techniques  
- Achieve optimal baking results using a home oven  
- Maintain food safety standards throughout preparation  
- Complete preparation within 90 minutes  
- Serve pizzas at appropriate temperatures

## 6. Materials and Ingredients (For 4 People)

### Dough Ingredients

| Ingredient         | Quantity |
|-------------------|----------|
| All-purpose flour | 3 cups   |
| Warm water        | 1 cup    |
| Active dry yeast  | 2¼ tsp   |
| Sugar             | 2 tsp    |
| Salt              | 1 tsp    |
| Olive oil         | 2 tbsp   |

---

### Tomato Sauce

| Ingredient        | Quantity |
|------------------|----------|
| Crushed tomatoes | 1 cup    |
| Garlic (minced)  | 2 cloves |
| Oregano          | 1 tsp    |
| Basil            | 1 tsp    |
| Salt             | ½ tsp    |
| Olive oil        | 1 tbsp   |

---

### Veggie Pizza Toppings

| Ingredient        | Quantity |
|------------------|----------|
| Onion            | 1 medium |
| Green pepper     | 1 medium |
| Tomato           | 1 medium |
| Mushrooms        | 1 cup    |
| Black olives     | ½ cup    |
| Mozzarella       | 2 cups   |

---

### BBQ Chicken Pizza Toppings

| Ingredient              | Quantity |
|------------------------|----------|
| Cooked chicken         | 1.5 cups |
| BBQ sauce              | ½ cup    |
| Red onion              | 1 medium |
| Green pepper           | 1 medium |
| Cheddar cheese         | 2 cups   |

---

## 7. Procedure

### 7.1 Dough Preparation

1. Activate yeast by combining warm water, yeast, and sugar. Allow to foam.  
2. Mix flour, salt, and sugar in a bowl.  
3. Add yeast mixture and oil; mix and knead until smooth.  
4. Allow dough to rise until doubled, then divide into two portions.

---

### 7.2 Sauce Preparation

- Combine crushed tomatoes, garlic, oregano, basil, salt, and olive oil  
- Allow sauce to rest  
- Prepare BBQ sauce separately

---

### 7.3 Veggie Pizza Assembly

1. Roll dough into a 12-inch circle  
2. Spread tomato sauce (⅓ cup)  
3. Add mozzarella cheese  
4. Add vegetables evenly  
5. Top with cheese and seasoning

---

### 7.4 BBQ Chicken Pizza Assembly

1. Roll dough  
2. Spread BBQ sauce (¼ cup)  
3. Add cheddar cheese  
4. Add chicken and vegetables  
5. Top with cheese and drizzle BBQ sauce 

---

## 8. Baking and Serving

### Baking

- Temperature: 450°F (230°C)  
- Time: 10–15 minutes  
- Bake until crust is golden and cheese bubbles  

### Serving

1. Cool for 2–3 minutes  
2. Slice into 8 pieces  
3. Serve at 120–140°F

---

## 9. Quality and Safety

- Wash hands before and during cooking  
- Keep ingredients refrigerated below 40°F  
- Cook chicken to 165°F  
- Avoid cross-contamination  
- Clean all surfaces after use  
- Use oven mitts for safety

---

## 10. Conclusion

This SOP ensures consistent, safe, and efficient pizza preparation in a home kitchen. It produces two 12-inch pizzas serving approximately four individuals while maintaining quality and food safety standards.
